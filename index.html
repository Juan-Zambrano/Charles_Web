Charles Okpoko

Bio

Charles Okpoko is amongst the top powerlifters in the world. He is regarded by many as the future of powerlifting, due to his many accomplishments at a young age, such as a podium finish at the World Games, the highest stage any powerlifter could ever compete in. He is a member of the USA Powerlifting national team with multiple national records and world championship titles under his belt. Not only is he an exceptional athlete, he has the brains to match. Charles is a graduate of The University of Texas at Austin with a B.S. in Exercise Science and a Pre-Health Professions Certificate, as well as a NSCA Certified Personal Trainer. He has written and has been featured in fitness publications such as BarBend and the International Powerlifting Federation Magazine. He has also hosted seminars and workshops to help powerlifters hone their skills. Driven by a passion and dedication to the health, fitness, and the sport of powerlifting, he founded Okpoko Infinite Fitness to provide quality coaching to lifters alike seeking to reach new heights.
